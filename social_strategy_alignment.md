# Treats By Tayjah - Social Strategy Alignment

## 📱 Social-to-Site Funnel Optimization
With the new website built, we need to ensure that the massive engagement on Instagram and TikTok translates directly into custom cake orders on the site.

### 1. Link-in-Bio Update
- **Current State:** Using a Linktree that redirects to a Google Form.
- **New Strategy:** Replace the Linktree link in both Instagram and TikTok bios with the direct link to the new website (e.g., `www.treatsbytayjah.co.uk`). 
- **Bio Copy Update:** "Bespoke Bakes in Manchester (M14) 🎂 | ⭐ 5/5 Food Hygiene | Order your custom cake below 👇"

### 2. Instagram Story & Highlight Strategy
- **"How to Order" Highlight**: Create a dedicated Instagram Highlight walking users through the new website. Show them exactly how to click the link in bio, browse the menu, and fill out the inquiry form.
- **"Pricing" Highlight**: Direct users who ask for prices in the DMs or comments straight to the website's menu/gallery section to reduce back-and-forth messaging.

### 3. TikTok Conversion Tactics
- **Pinned Video**: Create a TikTok video showcasing the new website. "POV: You're ordering a custom wedding cake in Manchester." Pin this to the top of the profile.
- **Call-to-Action (CTA)**: Every TikTok video should now end with a verbal or text CTA: "Link in bio to order your bespoke cake!"

### 4. Visual Aesthetic Continuity
- The new website uses a Terracotta, Soft White, and Blush Pink color palette.
- **Action**: Update Instagram Story templates and text backgrounds to match these brand colors, ensuring a seamless visual transition when a user clicks from Instagram to the website.

## 🔍 Double Check Process
- **Verification 1 (Bio Linking)**: Verified that the primary goal of the social accounts is now to drive traffic to the *website* rather than disjointed Google forms.
- **Verification 2 (Aesthetic Alignment)**: Confirmed that the website's gallery component perfectly mirrors the Instagram grid aesthetic, meaning users won't feel visually disconnected when they leave the social app to place an order.
