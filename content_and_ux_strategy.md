# Brand & Content Strategy

## Brand Voice & Tone
- **Vibe:** Warm, inviting, elegant, and playful. The text should sound like a friendly, high-end neighborhood baker.
- **Tagline:** "Treats That Taste As Good As They Look!"
- **Primary Focus:** Highlighting the bespoke nature of the custom cakes, cupcakes, and cookies. 
- **Local Focus:** Manchester (M14) - ensure this is prominent for local SEO and customer trust.

## Core Copy Elements

### Homepage Hero Section
- **Headline:** Bespoke Bakes for Every Occasion in Manchester
- **Sub-headline:** From custom cakes to delicate cookies—"Treats That Taste As Good As They Look!"
- **Call to Action (CTA):** View Gallery / Request a Custom Order

### "About Tayjah" Section
- **Header:** Meet the Baker
- **Body:** Hi, I'm Tayjah! Based right here in Manchester (M14), I specialize in bringing your sweetest visions to life. Whether you're looking for an elegant tiered wedding cake, playful birthday cupcakes, or custom cookies for a corporate event, every single treat is baked from scratch with love and the finest ingredients in my 5/5 Food Hygiene Rated kitchen. 

### Custom Orders Description
- **Header:** How It Works
- **Body:** Got a specific design in mind? Or need some inspiration? I work closely with every client to create something uniquely tailored to your event. Simply fill out the inquiry form, let me know your date, theme, and serving size, and we'll start baking up some magic!

# UX Planning & Architecture (Sitemap)

## Structure
A clean, single-page scroll layout (or very simple multi-page structure) to reduce friction and keep the focus on the visuals.

1. **Header / Navigation**
   - Logo (Treats By Tayjah)
   - Links: Gallery, Menu/Flavors, About, Order Now
2. **Hero Section**
   - High-quality background image/video of a signature cake.
   - Core headline and primary CTA (Order Now).
3. **Featured Treats (Menu Highlights)**
   - 3 columns featuring: Custom Cakes | Cupcakes | Cookies
   - Brief descriptions for each category.
4. **Visual Gallery (Instagram/TikTok Feed)**
   - Grid layout showing the latest posts to keep content fresh without manual website updates.
5. **About Section**
   - Brief bio and photo of Tayjah.
6. **Custom Order Form / Inquiry Section**
   - Simple form: Name, Email, Date of Event, Description of what they want, Reference Image upload (if possible).
7. **Footer**
   - Links to TikTok, Instagram, Facebook.
   - Location: Manchester, M14.
   - Contact Email.

## User Journey
- **Social Follower:** Clicks the link in TikTok/IG bio -> lands on Hero Section -> scrolls to see structured offerings -> goes to the Order Form.
- **Local Searcher:** Googles "Custom cakes Manchester M14" -> lands on Hero Section -> scrolls to see Gallery to verify quality -> goes to the Order Form.
