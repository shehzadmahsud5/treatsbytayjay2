## 🎨 Design Foundations

### Color System
**Primary Colors**: 
- **Terracotta/Warm Chocolate** (`#8B4513`): Used for primary buttons, prominent text, and call-to-actions. Highly appetizing and elegant.
**Secondary Colors**: 
- **Pastel Blush Pink** (`#FDF3F1`): Used for secondary section backgrounds (e.g., the "About Tayjah" block) to break up the page softly.
**Neutral Palette**: 
- **Soft Warm White** (`#FAF8F5`): The primary background color. Much softer on the eyes than pure white, giving a premium, boutique bakery feel.
- **Deep Charcoal** (`#2D2D2D`): Used for all primary body text to ensure maximum readability without the harshness of pure black.
**Accessibility**: All text over backgrounds maintains a minimum WCAG AA 4.5:1 contrast ratio.

### Typography System
**Primary Font**: **Outfit** or **Playfair Display** (Elegant, modern serif/sans-serif hybrid) for all headlines (H1, H2, H3).
**Secondary Font**: **Inter** (Clean, highly readable sans-serif) for all body copy and form inputs.
**Font Weights**: 400 (Regular) for body, 600 (Semi-bold) for CTAs, 700 (Bold) for headlines.

## 🧱 Component Library

### Base Components
**Buttons**: 
- **Primary**: Solid Terracotta background, White text. Deep hover shadow. Used for "Order Now".
- **Secondary**: Transparent background, Terracotta outline. Used for "View Gallery".
**Trust Badges**:
- **Hygiene Rating Badge**: A clean, circular icon for the "5/5 Food Hygiene Rating" placed prominently in the hero section and footer.

### Component States
**Interactive States**: 
- **Hover**: Buttons will slightly elevate (translateY: -2px) and gain a soft drop-shadow (`box-shadow: 0 4px 6px rgba(0,0,0,0.1)`) to encourage clicking.
- **Form Focus**: When a user clicks to type in the order form, a soft Terracotta outline will highlight the active field.

## ♿ Accessibility Standards
- **Keyboard Navigation**: Full functionality without a mouse; focus rings visible.
- **Touch Targets**: All buttons and mobile navigation items are at least 44px tall, ensuring they are easy to tap on phones.
